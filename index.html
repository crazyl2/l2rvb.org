Eventos de Batalha

Time contra Time (TVT)

Guerra total de facções com possíveis chefes PvP dinâmicos neste evento.

Rei da Colina (KOTH)

Controle as zonas centrais para ganhar adena e recompensas. Um ataque zumbi pode tomar o centro.

Campo de Batalha

Domine territórios com sua facção, defenda-os e obtenha recompensas.

Batalha pelo Castelo

Ataque ou defenda o castelo, domine-o com sua equipe e receba todas as recompensas. Os guardas o defenderão.

Mini Events (5 Minutes)

Death Match

The player with the most kills wins a special reward. Every kill grants Adena and materials.

Bow Event

Run and kill with reduced speed. Each opponent kill grants rewards.

Mob Waves

Fight waves of monsters. Each mob drops rewards. Reach the final stage to earn a team prize.

Useful Commands

.map Shows remaining time, current event, and map.
.points Shows your faction points and all factions' scores.
.buffsummonfighter Buff your pet/summon as a fighter.
.buffsummonmage Buff your pet/summon as a mage.
.vip Check your VIP status and remaining time.

Progression

Start at Level 76

Free Grade A gear. No grinding required. server is 30x Exp gained by pvp and mobs

Earn in Combat

Kill enemies Mob and PvP to get Adena, Coal, Varnish, and more.

Enchant System

Safe +3. Max: +16 (weapons), +12 (armor & jewelry).
Scroll rates: 55% Normal, 65% Blessed, 100% Crystal.

Classes

Support classes are essential. They provide critical buffs like Chant of Victory, Prophecy, and elemental chants (Water, Fire, Wind). Buffs are unlocked by leveling up your support class. All players benefit from up to 20 buff slots.

Recompensas

Adena e Adena Antiga

Obtida por abates, captura de bandeiras e vitória em eventos.

Caixa Misteriosa

Contém materiais e pergaminhos raros.

Caixa de Armas

Concede armas e equipamentos de alto nível.

Melhores Abates

Os 3 melhores jogadores recebem equipamentos exclusivos e caixas.
